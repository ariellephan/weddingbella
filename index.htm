What is this project?

A photographer and venue "almanac", a place for photographer to showcase their work with tags of location, venue, style and budget AND for us to find the talents who can capture our beautiful, if not the most important, moments in our lives.

2.

Why is it needed?

Whether it is a party, portrait, wedding, engagement or honeymoon, everyone found themselves searching for a good photographer at least once. We make it easier to find one and help you discover places you may never hear about. Current wedding websites are crowded with bazillions of other information and we try to do ONE thing and be good at it.
